Deluxe Room

This 34 sq. m guestroom features a king-size bed or two single beds. Its cozy ambiance offers privacy and relaxation away from the hustle and bustle.

Junior Suite

These strategically located corner suites offer a spacious living area. At 43 sq. m, it has ample space to move around and floor-to-ceiling glass windows to view the city.

Executive Suite

Built especially for long-staying guests, this suite is equipped with a kitchenette that has an induction cooker and a refrigerator.

Bay Suite

Named as such because of its spectacular view of Laguna de Bay, this suite offers comfortable living. Its sleek interiors provide the important necessities with a bedroom, living area, kitchenette and patio.

Commerce Suite

A one-bedroom suite tastefully designed for the modern traveler, it is suited to welcome guests with its inviting dining and living area as well as its pristine kitchenette. Step out of the patio and take in the breathtaking view of the city lights.

Acacia Suite

The Commerce Suite and Bay Suite combined gives our guests 178 sq.m of splendid space. The suite has two bedrooms, kitchenettes, living rooms, a dining room and outdoor patios that are fully furnished for that sophisticated living experience.
